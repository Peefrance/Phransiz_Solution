About Francis Peter

Alt School ID: ALT/SOE/024/0440

Biography:

Francis Peter is a committed and dedicated Electrical engineer with over three years of hands-on practical experience in planned and corrective maintenance. Armed with a degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the Federal University of Technology Minna, he has contributed to a number of projects within the organizations where he has worked and has proffered real-time working solutions for complicated problems. He is interested in diversifying his skills and career towards Software Engineering and is interested in working and collaborating with like-minded individuals in Tech who are looking forward to proffering solutions to present challenges that will assist humans in needed circumstances. He will continue to explore new technologies and software packages, and continue to seek research opportunities and lifelong learning avenues to grow his skills and career in the aspects of Tech.
